# Kedgerie

## Ingredients
4 eggs boiled 7 min  
2 cups rice  
red salmon 415g  
butter  
onion  
tumeric  
curry powder  
ginger, garlic  
2 red chilli  
capsicum  
corriander  

## method
1. in a wok, low heat saute ginger, garlic, onion
2. add chilli, capsicum & spices
3. add salmon then rice, increase heat
4. add corriander & sliced egg
