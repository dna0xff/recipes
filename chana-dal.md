# Chana Dal
## Ingredients
1 cup Chana Dal
4 cup water
1 tomato

### Tempur
6 cloves
garam masala
6 cloves garlic, finely sliced
cillantro


# Method
1. soak dal at least 1 hr, pref longer
2. heat dal w water in pan 20 min
3. combine other ingredients w 1/2 cup water then add to pan

## Tempur
heat oil, add cloves & garlic
