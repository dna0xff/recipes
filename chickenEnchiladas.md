## Ingredients
* 1 red onion
* 4 cloves garlic

* 2 tsp chicken stock powder in 1/2 cup water
* 4 chicken breast fillets
* 12 soft tortillas

### Salsa
* 1 red onion
* 2 small red capsicum
* 60g corn kernels
* 1/2 cup fresh corriander
* 8 tsp lime juice
