### Chicken & Leek pie
This is wickedly delicous mainly because of the cream.

#### Ingredients

##### Crust
* 1 1/2 cups plain flour
* 90 gm cold butter
* 1 egg
* 1 tsp iced water

##### Filling
* 1tbs olive oil
* 800 gm chicken breast
* 2 rashes bacon ( 130 gm ) - slice thin 
* 1 Leek - slice thin
* 1 cup dry white wine
* 2 sheets of puff pastry for top ( same for bottom ) 
* 20 gm butter,
* 1 tbs flour
* 300 ml cream
* 1/2 cup milk
* 1 egg
