Welcome, this is pretty simple.

  1. Press Stby to capture an initial position.
  2. Get your vessel on to the desired heading, trim etc.
  3. Settings for desired checking interval and off-course limit.
  4. press Run to capture the current position. A heading will be calculated and a timer started.
  5. When the timer goes off the current position will be updated and the course checked against the heading from earlier.
  6. If am alarm condition exists, the screen will flash and the klaxon will sound.
  7. Dismiss the alarm will stop the noise BUT the alarm condition still exists and will trigger again next check.
  8. can either change settings, go to standby or turn off
